About JFM Consult

Jessica Ocquaye

Life and Career Coach

I believe that our passion is a core driving force that ignites us to fulfil our purpose and sometimes we might just need help resetting our compasses to get back on ‘our’ path. I think the key thing is about re-connecting with the essence of who we truly are.

My approach is relatively simple. I will not profess to be a miracle worker, however I am committed to ensuring that anyone I work with moves from where they are now to where they want to be, understanding that we are all on a unique and different journey.
I believe we can begin to progress when we are clear on where it is where we are going, and hold on to how we expect to feel when we arrive there.
I have a transpersonal coaching style as the crux of what I aspire is to do is connecting people to their purpose and helping them discover meaning.

As well as coaching, other services offered include the following:

  • Strengthscope Practitioner
  • Design OD Interventions and Team away days
  • Leadership development programme design
  • Learning and development programme design
  • Powerpoint presentation design - Turn your content into great presentations
  • Organisation Development Consultancy

So why not get in touch today to discuss your needs.
